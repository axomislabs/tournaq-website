Social Scramble

Social Scramble is a rotating partner format where players are reshuffled into new teams at the start of each round. No two people should be paired together more than once. The result is a social event where everyone interacts with everyone — competitive enough to be engaging, but designed to mix the group rather than crown a winner.

Players
4 – 32+
Teams per round
2 per court
Player assignment
Randomised
Tournament required
Yes

How it works

The organiser sets up the event and registers all players before the event starts. Available time can be considered as well as match length. Play tournaments between 15 min or multiple hours, you choose. TournaQ generates a match schedule where partners rotate each round, minimising repeat pairings. Scores are recorded per match. At the end of all rounds, individual standings are calculated based on personal win/point totals — because in a scramble, you compete as an individual across changing teams.

Best for

  • Club social days and open events
  • Mixed-ability groups where balanced competition matters
  • Events where meeting new players is part of the fun
  • Beach volleyball clinics and training days with multiple participants
