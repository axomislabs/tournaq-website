Match Scoring

Core point tracking, set management, and score correction are live. Timeout tracking, player statistics, and match analytics are in active development.

  • Available
    Live point-by-point scoring

    Tap to award points to either team in real time. The interface is optimised for one-handed use during active play.

  • Available
    Set management

    Automatic set tracking with configurable winning conditions. TournaQ handles the transition between sets without manual input.

  • Available
    Score correction

    Undo the last point at any time. Mistakes during fast play can be corrected immediately without losing match state.

  • Planned
    Timeout tracking

    Record timeouts per team per set, in line with standard beach volleyball rules.

  • Available
    Match result & history

    Completed matches are saved locally on the device with full score history per set.

  • Planned
    Player point statistics

    Track individual player contributions within a match — points won, errors, and serve effectiveness.

  • Planned
    Historical match analytics

    Aggregated statistics and trends across matches and seasons for teams and individual players.
